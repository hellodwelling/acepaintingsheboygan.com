Our Services

What we are best at

Exterior Painting

If the home is where the heart is, then you want your home to look great. It all starts with a fantastic exterior. From exterior touchups to full home recoating we cover it all.

Interior Painting

Time to get rid of the crayon marks and dings in the walls with a fresh coat. Whether you want to simple refresh a room or pick a totally new color we can help.

Kitchen and Cabinet Refinishing

You don't need to replace cabinets as soon as you might think. We can make your old cabinets look like new for a fraction of the cost!

Drywall and Texture

Getting wall scars and dings back to looking like new.

Pressure Washing

From patios to home exteriors pressure washing can bring the life back to your property. Remove dirt that builds up in the harsh Wisconsin climate in a few hours.

Stains

For your outdoor patio, decks and more we are experts at identifying and applying stains that will not only look great but protect against the elements.
